About ENSTA

Welcome to ENSTA Dergana

ENSTA Dergana is a prestigious institution of higher education operating under the supervision of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. It is dedicated to training highly qualified engineers and architects capable of meeting the demands of both national and international socio-economic sectors.

Equipped with modern facilities and supported by a committed faculty, the school offers students a solid foundation of theoretical knowledge combined with practical experience, ensuring they are well-prepared for the challenges of the professional world.

The institution emphasizes innovation, critical thinking, and leadership, fostering the development of future technological and architectural leaders. ENSTA Dergana also provides a dynamic and stimulating campus environment that encourages both academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Departments

Cybersecurity

Protecting digital assets and information systems from cyber threats through advanced security protocols and technologies. Students learn about network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking.

Artificial Intelligence

Developing intelligent systems and algorithms that can learn, reason, and solve complex problems autonomously. Focus areas include machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

Electrical Engineering

Designing and implementing electrical systems, power distribution networks, and electronic devices. Students work with circuits, power systems, and renewable energy technologies.

Mechanical Engineering

Creating mechanical systems and devices through design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance. Curriculum covers thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and materials science.

Industrial Engineering

Optimizing complex processes and systems to improve efficiency and productivity in industrial settings. Students learn about operations research, quality control, and supply chain management.

Logistics Engineering

Managing the flow of goods, information, and resources through supply chain optimization and transportation systems. Focus on inventory management, distribution, and logistics technology.

Process Engineering

Designing, operating, and optimizing chemical and physical processes for industrial applications. Students study reaction engineering, separation processes, and process control.

Industrial Computing

Integrating computing systems with industrial processes for automation and control applications. Curriculum includes PLC programming, industrial networks, and SCADA systems.

Computer Engineering Program

The Computer Engineering (MI) program at ENSTA Dergana is designed to equip students with the technical knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computing and information technology.

Website Design Advice

Things to Do

  1. Plan your content structure - Create a clear information architecture before designing.
  2. Prioritize mobile responsiveness - Ensure your site works well on all devices.
  3. Optimize for performance - Compress images and minimize code for faster loading.
  4. Use consistent branding - Maintain consistent colors, fonts, and design elements.
  5. Implement intuitive navigation - Make it easy for users to find what they need.
  6. Include clear calls-to-action - Guide users toward desired actions.
  7. Ensure accessibility - Make your site usable for people with disabilities.
  8. Test thoroughly - Check functionality across different browsers and devices.
  9. Optimize for SEO - Use proper headings, meta tags, and semantic HTML.
  10. Keep content updated - Regularly refresh content to maintain relevance.

Things to Avoid

  1. Don't overload with animations - Use animations sparingly to avoid distraction.
  2. Avoid cluttered layouts - Use white space effectively for better readability.
  3. Don't use too many fonts - Stick to 2-3 complementary fonts maximum.
  4. Avoid auto-playing media - Let users control audio and video playback.
  5. Don't ignore loading times - Users will leave if pages load too slowly.
  6. Avoid broken links - Regularly check and fix any broken navigation.
  7. Don't use unclear navigation - Make menu labels descriptive and intuitive.
  8. Avoid inaccessible color contrasts - Ensure text is readable against backgrounds.
  9. Don't forget about security - Implement HTTPS and protect against vulnerabilities.
  10. Avoid outdated design trends - Keep your design modern but timeless.
